There are several ways to discover new music on Spotify. You can explore curated playlists under the "Browse" section, check out personalized recommendations on the "Discover" tab, and listen to radio stations based on your favorite artists or genres. Additionally, you can follow friends or public playlists to find fresh tracks.
Yes, there is a free version of Spotify. With the free plan, you can listen to music, create playlists, and discover new tracks. However, you'll hear ads between songs, and you won't have the ability to download content for offline listening. If you want an ad-free experience with offline access, you can consider upgrading to Spotify Premium.
